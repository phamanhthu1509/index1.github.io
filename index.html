About us

Vincom Center for Contemporary Art (VCCA) is a non-profit art center sponsored by Vingroup, with the mission of connecting artists with the community; works with lovers of Beauty; Vietnamese contemporary art with world contemporary art. The center develops on the basis of diverse art activities with international standard infrastructure and equipment, converging talented and enthusiastic artists at home and abroad. VCCA aims to be one of the important art centers of Vietnam and Southeast Asian countries.
